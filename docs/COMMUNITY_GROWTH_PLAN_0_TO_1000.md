## FAQ

**Q: What if I don't have time for 90 min/day?**
A: Start with 30 min/day minimum. Post once, reply to 10 accounts. Quality > quantity.

**Q: Should I use Twitter Ads?**
A: Not yet. Organic first. Once you hit 500 followers, consider $100 test budget.

**Q: What if my tweets get no engagement?**
A: Keep going. First 100 followers are the hardest. Improve content, increase replies.

**Q: Should I focus on Twitter or build the product?**
A: Both. Spend 80% on product, 20% on community. This plan is the 20%.

**Q: When should I launch Discord?**
A: Week 3-4 (once you hit 300-500 followers). Not before.

**Q: Do I need to post every day?**
A: Yes. Minimum 1x daily. Ideally 2x. More than 4x = diminishing returns.

**Q: What if I'm not good at writing?**
